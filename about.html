The Concept

By using the Pomodoro technique Domada is able to manage your time and brain capacity easily and efficiently. Your time is split in well-defined units, and breaks are allowed, even encouraged to regenerate your mental powers.

The concept is simple: You work for 25 minutes, uninterrupted, on the task at hand. After that, you get five minutes to do whatever you want. It is neccessary to not work in this time, to free your mind of whats been done before. After the break, work is resumed for the next "Pomodoro".

After four Pomodoros, it is time to take a bigger break of fifteen minutes.

By allowing your brain to cool down, but on the other hand to focus for quite some time you can achieve more than you thought you could, and have fun at the same time!

Data Protection Policy

Domada takes great care to make sure your privacy is protected.

Our Data Protection Policy is simple: No usage data is collected, and absolutely no data is given to third-parties.

Note that this policy does not account for data collected by your browser, your employer, your ISP etc.
